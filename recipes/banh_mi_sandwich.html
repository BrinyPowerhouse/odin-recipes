Bánh Mì Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette or French roll
  • bietje cooked and sliced pork (can also use chicken, tofu, or beef)
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha or other chili sauce
  • some hoisin sauce
  • some sprinkles of maggi
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced pickled carrots or daikon radish
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, thinly sliced (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cut the baguette or French roll in half lengthwise, and then toast the bread in the oven until lightly crispy.
  2. heat the meat in a skillet, add the Sriracha, hoisin and maggi and mix
  3. Spread mayonnaise sauce on the bottom half of the bread.
  4. Layer the sliced pork, pickled carrots and daikon radish, cilantro, mint, basil and jalapeño pepper (if using) on top of the mayonnaise mixture.
  5. Close the sandwich and cut it into halves or quarters.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Note: You can also add pate, butter and soy sauce to make it more flavorful.

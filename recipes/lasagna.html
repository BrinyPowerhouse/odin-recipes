Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 450 grams ground beef or Italian sausage (or a combination of both)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 796 grams can crushed tomatoes
  • 170 grams can tomato paste
  • 120 milliliters of water
  • 30 grams of chopped fresh basil leaves
  • 5 grams dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 450 grams lasagna noodles
  • 454 grams ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 30 grams of chopped fresh parsley
  • 907 grams shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 50 grams grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef (or sausage) until browned. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and let cook for about 20 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and some salt and pepper.
  7. Spread a small amount of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 23x33 cm baking dish.
  8. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the meat sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Repeat layering the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese until all ingredients are used up. The top layer should be a generous amount of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  11. Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese on top is golden brown and bubbly.
  13. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
